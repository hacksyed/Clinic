What is All-On-6 Dental Implant Surgery?

An all-on-6 dental implant is similar to All-on-4, here we use 6 dental implants in place of 4 unlike in All-On-4 implants.



Why All-On-4 Dental Implant Surgery?

All-on-4 dental implants are done in very less time with less discomfort and are more cost-effective. By tilting posterior implants, the All-on-4 dental implant surgery maximises the use of available bone on the anterior jaw and allows for immediate function. All-on-4 dental implant surgery gets over in a single day and depending on the patient it often ranges from 2 to 4 hours.



How Does All-On-6 Dental Implant Surgery Works?

The All-On-6 Dental Implant Surgery gives a permanent prosthesis with six dental implants. These six dental implants act as the anchor for a bridge and are implanted in the lower or upper jaw to permanently anchor prosthetic teeth in place. We evaluate the anatomical structures in the mouth by conducting Cone Beam Computer Tomogram (CB-CT) scans and intra-oral scans.



Before All-On-6 Dental Implant Surgery

Depending on the bone density of the patient, grafting is done if needed. We examine and evaluate your mouth and advise you accordingly before starting the All-On-6 Dental Implant Surgery.



During All-On-6 Dental Implant Surgery

At first, you will be sedated with local anaesthesia or intravenous sedation. Next, we will insert the dental implants into the jaw bone. We attach a temporary prosthesis until the dental implants are healed.



After All-On-6 Dental Implant Surgery

It might take around 3 months to heal completely depending on your jaw strength. During this time, the jaw bone and gums shape themselves and bond with the new dental implants. You need to take utmost care till they are completely healed. Once the dental implants are completely fused into the jawline a permanent prosthesis is sealed on the dental implants.



Benefits of All-on-6 Dental Implant Surgery

All-on-6 dental implant surgery requires only 6 dental implants to have a complete arch of teeth per jaw rather than 10 - 12 implants in traditional dental implants.

All-on-6 dental implants require only one surgery.

All-on-6 dental implants are often regarded as the most effective replacement for dentures or damaged teeth with a 98% success rate.

To complete all-on-6 dental implant surgery for one arch takes not more than 3.0 hrs.

In All-on-6 dental implant surgery, the prosthesis is firmly fixed into place at an angle to allow more contact support with your natural bone and as a result bone grafting is not necessary.

After having All-on-6 dental implant surgery one can recover within a week. As there is no need for additional surgeries and follow-up appointments you can save money and time as well.



How to Clean All-On-6 Dental Implants?

You must keep your new teeth clean and clear from bacteria and plaque for your implants to last long. They can induce infection in the tissues and are the most common causes of implant failure. The maintenance of proper oral hygiene cannot be overstated.



Effective hygienic tips include:

  • Brushing with a soft-bristled toothbrush at least twice a day is recommended.
  • Flossing with unwaxed tape or implant-specific floss on a daily basis.
  • Using a nylon coated brush, clean in between teeth and hard to reach areas.
  • Should not use highly abrasive toothpaste.


When To Choose All-On-6 Dental Implants Over All-On-4 Dental Implants?

All-on-6 implants are a better choice only if a more extensive reconstruction is required as the additional implants provide additional support. You could be a better candidate for All-on-4 if you don't have the extra bone mass required for the additional implants.
